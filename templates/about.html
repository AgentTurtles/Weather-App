About Weather App

Weather App is your one-stop solution for real-time and future weather updates for any city in the world. Get detailed forecasts, discover weather in popular cities, and use your current location for local weather insights. Built with Flask, Bootstrap, and OpenWeatherMap.

  • Real-time weather data
  • 3-day future forecast
  • Popular cities' weather
  • Location-based suggestions
  • Modern, responsive design
